OUR STORY

Founded in the early 1960s, Bharat Tyres is a highly respected and successful tyre dealer in India. The company's foundation is built on a set of strong values, including discipline, determination, dedication, and strategic decision-making. With honesty, transparency, and integrity as cornerstones, Bharat Tyres has become the largest tyre distributor in India, renowned for its expertise, reliability, and innovative approach to the industry.

Our Management

Shree Mahendra Verma

Motabhai, Bhaisaheb's older son, joined the company in 1976, expanding the family legacy through the launch of additional retail businesses in different districts of Gujarat. His business acumen has played a vital role in driving the company's success today.

Shree Kalpesh Verma

Bhaisaheb's son joined Bharat Tyres in 1987 and revolutionized the company with digital technology. He streamlined accounting, finance, HR, and IT processes and continues to innovate with boundless imagination and relentless effort.

Shree Harsh Verma

In 2007, Shree Harsh Verma, the son of Mahendra Verma, joined Bharat Tyres as the third-generation business owner. As Sales and Marketing Director, he brings a forward-thinking approach to the company, leveraging his strong business and networking skills to excel in his role.
