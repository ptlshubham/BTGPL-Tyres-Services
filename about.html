OUR STORY

Successful business since 1963

Founded in 1963 by the visionary entrepreneur Shree Bishamberdayal Chiranjilal Verma, Bharat Tyres has grown into a highly respected tyre dealer in the industry. Here are the key factors that have contributed to their success:

  • Strong moral values: Bharat Tyres is built on a foundation of Discipline, Determination, Dedication, and Decision - values that have guided the company's culture and growth.
  • Resilience: Overcoming numerous challenges and struggles, Bharat Tyres has proved to be a company that can weather any storm and emerge victorious.
  • Inspiring leadership: Bhaisaheb's inspiring leadership and drive for success has been the cornerstone of the company's growth.
  • Fair business practices: Bharat Tyres conducts its business with honesty, transparency, and integrity, earning the trust of customers and suppliers alike.

Today, Bharat Tyres stands tall as a symbol of hard work, determination, and strong values - an inspiring success story that continues to inspire many.

Our Management

Shree Mahendra Verma

Bhaisaheb's elder brother joined the company in 1976, maintaining the family legacy. "Motabhai" launched additional retail businesses in different Gujarat districts to ensure business continuity and expansion outside of Anand. He inherited his father's greatness.

Shree Kalpesh Verma

Bhaisaheb's younger son joined Bharat Tyres in 1987 and deployed digital technology throughout the company. He also knows accounting, auditing, and computers. He's always seeking better company processes. His imagination is boundless.
