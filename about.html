OUR STORY

Successful business since 1963

Bharat Tyres' success story began in 1963, when a visionary entrepreneur Shree Bishamberdayal Chiranjilal Verma founded this company in 150 sq. ft. shop in the small town of Anand, Gujarat. Shree Bishamberdayal Chiranjilal Verma emerged as a winner despite numerous challenges and struggles over the years due to his strong will and business ethics.

He has truly transformed his business dream into a successful empire that stands tall on four pillars: Discipline, Determination, Dedication, and Decision. These moral virtues are significant reasons for Bharat Tyres' success in a small town with few business opportunities. Today, Bhaisaheb's inspiring leadership, drive for success, and fair and transparent business practises have elevated us to the ranks of the most renowned and respected tyre dealers in the competitive market.

Journey towards Excellence

Over the course of our great journey of more than 60 years, we have had numerous highs and lows, incredible leaders, important turning points, and amazing successes. Bhaisaheb formed partnerships with some of the most famous companies in the tyre industry throughout the years, including Goodyear, Premier, and CEAT, by drawing on his extensive expertise and wealth of experience in the tyre industry.With more than 1000 business partners in the state's major municipalities, we have now extended our operations across the entire state of Gujarat. Our most recent endeavour is to expand our operations throughout India. We have used cutting-edge technical infrastructure for logistics, including SAP and ERP platform, to provide the greatest client service. Bharat Tyres, a sincere and trustworthy partner that CEAT discovered, has given us a dealership in central and southern Gujarat. We have enjoyed a fruitful and prosperous relationship for more than 50 years, and we are extremely delighted to announce that we now hold the prestigious title of

"CEAT's Largest Business Partner Worldwide"

which is an accomplishment unheard of for a company headquartered in a tiny town of Gujarat.

Our Management

Shree Mahendra Verma

In 1976, Bhaisaheb's oldest brother, Shree Mahendra Verma, joined the company, carrying on a family tradition. He was known as "Motabhai," and he was very important to the growth and continuation of business outside of Anand. He did this by starting new retail businesses in several places in Gujarat.

He is just like his great father in all the best ways.
Shree Kalpesh Verma

When Bhaisaheb's younger son Shree Kalpesh Verma joined Bharat Tyres in 1987, he implemented digital technology throughout the company's operations. Additionally, he has strong accounting, auditing, and computer skills. He is constantly looking for new and improved ways to carry out tasks within the company.

His passion and creativity know no bounds
Shree Harsh Verma

Shree Harsh Verma, the son of Shree Mahendra Verma, who joined the organisation in 2007, is a representative of the third generation of business owners. He is a vibrant and outspoken person with a forward-thinking business outlook. He is the organization's sales and marketing director.

His business knowledge and ability to make connections are unmatched.

At Bharat Tyres, we strongly believe that “a great company is a result of the collective efforts of great people.” Bhaisaheb has always treated his employees as a part of his own family. Their hard work and loyalty is truly appreciated and rewarded at Bharat Tyres.

We have highly knowledgeable, experienced and talented team of professionals working with us who have given their best performance to make this company reach where it is today. Be it sales, administration, finance or technical assistance; our executives are the super stars of our success story.
