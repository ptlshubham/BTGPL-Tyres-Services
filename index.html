Our History

Bharat Tyres' success story began in 1963, when a visionary entrepreneur Shree Bishamberdayal Chiranjilal Verma founded this company in a 150 sq. ft. shop in the small town of Anand, Gujarat. Shree Bishamberdayal Chiranjilal Verma emerged as a winner despite numerous challenges and struggles over the years due to his strong will and business ethics.

He has truly transformed his business dream into a successful empire that stands tall on four pillars: Discipline, Determination, Dedication, and Decision. These moral virtues are significant reasons for Bharat Tyres' success in a small town with few business opportunities.
