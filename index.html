Our History

In 1963, Shree Bishamberdayal Chiranjilal Verma started Bharat Tyres in a tiny 150 sq. ft. shop in Anand, Gujarat. Despite facing numerous challenges and struggles, his determination and strong business ethics helped him turn his business dream into a successful empire.

Bharat Tyres Gujarat Private Limited (BTGPL) - the company's legal name is a testament to the power of four moral pillars that form the backbone of the company's success. These are:

  • Discipline: Bharat Tyres adheres to strict principles of order and control that govern their operations, helping them to maintain high levels of efficiency and productivity.
  • Determination: The company has shown remarkable resilience in the face of adversity, overcoming obstacles to emerge victorious time and time again.
  • Dedication: The team at Bharat Tyres is deeply committed to their work, going above and beyond to deliver exceptional products and services to their customers.
  • Decision: The company has always been guided by a strong sense of purpose, making strategic decisions that have helped them to stay ahead of the curve in a highly competitive industry.

BTGPL may be the company's legal name, but Bharat Tyres Gujarat - the company's trade name - is a name that is synonymous with excellence in the tyre industry. Their inspiring success story is a testament to the power of hard work, determination, and unwavering moral values.
