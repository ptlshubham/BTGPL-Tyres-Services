Our History

In 1963, Shree Bishamberdayal Chiranjilal Verma founded Bharat Tyres in a small 150 sq. ft. shop in Anand, Gujarat. Despite facing various challenges, his strong determination and business ethics helped him transform his dream into a successful empire.

Today, Bharat Tyres Gujarat Private Limited (BTGPL) is a leading name in the tyre industry and one of the largest tyre distributors in India, renowned for its commitment to excellence. We have achieved this success by adhering to four key principles:

  • GROWTH MINDSET

    We are constantly seeking to improve and grow, making strategic decisions that keep us ahead of the curve.

  • INTEGRITY & DISCIPLINE

    We adhere to strict principles of order and control, ensuring our operations are efficient and productive.

  • CUSTOMER-CENTRICITY

    Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional products and services, always putting our customers at the forefront.

  • RESILIENCE

    We have overcome numerous obstacles and challenges, showing remarkable resilience and emerging victorious time and time again.
