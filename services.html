OUR SERVICES

CEAT is not a brand that was originally from India, despite what its widespread appeal may lead one to believe. However, over the course of its existence, it has perfected both the value market and the products and services it provides. It was founded in India in 1979 and is now a subsidiary of RPG Group, which was founded in India. CEAT has been in business since 1958 and today provides a variety of tyres for many types of vehicles, including passenger cars, two-wheelers, SUVs and pickups, agricultural vehicles, earth-movers and construction equipment, and commercial vehicles.

At the moment, the business manufactures more than 15 million tyres on an annual basis. CEAT maintains operations in about 130 countries across the globe, with its headquarters located in Mumbai. Its tyres are manufactured in a number of plants located in Ambernath, Halol, Bhandup, Nagpur, Chennai, and Nashik in the Indian subcontinent. The organisation works with more than 4500 different dealers all around India and Bharat Tyres is one of them. In addition, it is a brand that is utilised frequently in the OE market.

Retreading

We've come a long way together in more than 60 years. We put money into retreading tyres not only because it seemed like a good idea, but also because it is a more advanced and environmentally friendly way to use tyres overall. To create and improve ring retread technology, we came up with the RINGTREAD System. Over the past sixty years, we've gained a lot of knowledge about how to supply and handle a complete Retreading System.

Today, we can say that we made the right choice, a choice that has travelled billions of kilometres around the entire planet. Bharat Tyres is a company that can give its customers different and flexible solutions for retreading plants of any size, level of automation, and amount of production.
